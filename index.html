Indian Chemical Society

Indian Chemical Society, one of the oldest pride nationalistic establishments in India, created with Acharya Prafulla Chandra Ray, the doyen of chemical sciences in India, as the Founder President with support of illustrious chemists like Professor J. C. Ghosh, Professor J. N. Mukherjee and Professor S. S. Bhatnagar on May 09, 1924. The Society is exercising its academic activity with the publication of a peer reviewed SCI Indexed Journal entitled “The Journal of the Indian Chemical Society” along with a periodical entitled, “Education in Chemical Science and Technology” . The Society organizes National and International Seminars and Chemists Convention in different institutes of the country. There are eighteen Endowment Awards in the name of illustrious chemists of the country, Acharya P. C. Ray Memorial Award, P. Ray Memorial Award, Professor J. C. Ghosh Memorial Award, Professor J. N. Mukherjee Award etc. along with Life Time Achievement Award. The selection is made through recommendation of National Selection Committee. For Young Chemists the Society has instituted Convention Award selected during Annual Convention of Chemists. Lectureship Programme is organized in Colleges to popularize chemistry amongst the students’ and also different chemistry competition on “Chemistry for Better Tomorrow” is regularly organizing in the midst of the School Children.
To celebrate the birth anniversary of the founder President, Acharya P. C. Ray, the Society organizes National Seminar for last decade. To commemorate his 157th Birth Anniversary the Society is going to organize a two day International Symposium on “Recent Advances in Molecules and Materials (RA2M–2018)” to be held on August 02 and 03, 2018 at the Auditorium of the Haldia Institute of Technology, Haldia, Purba Medinipur, West Bengal.

Haldia Institute of Technology

The pride establishment of West Bengal, Haldia Institute of Technology, An Institute of ICARE (Indian Center for the Advancement of Research and Education), Haldia, Approved by AICTE, Affiliated to MAKAUT)began its journey in the year 1996 and is dedicated to the service of the nation from its foundation. It is the first private and accredited academic institution catering technical education in West Bengal. The Institute is situated at Haldia – an industrial hub in Eastern India. Five B.Tech. (Undergraduate) Programs in Chemical Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Production Engineering and Department of Biotechnology are accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA) . The Institute has also been accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Coucil (NAAC) for five years w.e.f. 16-09-2016 with “A” Grade [CGPA-3.31] in 2nd Cycle and valid upto 15-09-2021. Since inception, Haldia Institute of Technology is dedicated to the objectives of creating highly trained professional manpower in various disciplines of Engineering, Technology and Social Science. The Institute is also dedicated to the contribution of higher scientific research in Technology as well as in Applied Science and Social Science.

About The Seminar

The seminar aims to provide a forum for eminent academicians, technologists, young researchers and students to exchange their ideas on the latest development and future trends in the fields of Synthesis, Characterizations and Technological Applications of Molecules & Materials. This seminar aims to bridge the gap between the eminent scientists and the young researchers/UG/PG students. UG/PG students will be encouraged to present (Research/Review based) scientific topics. The seminar will promote the Talent from School students to show their scientific projects, to encourage them to future research in the motto of ‘Science for Nation Building’. The programme will also include a quiz competition among school students to popularize chemistry and chemical science with a motto of “Chemistry for Better Tomorrow” ..
